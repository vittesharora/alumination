Outshine your future.

The 'Piece de Resistance' of SARC, Alumination is IIT Bombay's largest platform for bilateral exchage between the IITB alumni and students on a spectrum spanning Career Guidance, Corporate Exposure, Life Learning and Motivational Talks.

Events.

02

Workshops

Workshops: SARC brings for the IIT-B students Advanced Certified Workshops where they can be nourished with the advanced and core concepts that bleed in the fields of Finance, Consulting and Entrepreneurship by the notable Alumni of IIT-B.

Register at the link below!

03

Speed Mentoring

Speed Mentoring: “Life is but so much more than a series of conquests and influence!" Speed mentoring provides you the golden opportunity to get mentored by professional life coaches in a one to one session to ensure you understand yourself better and unlock your truest potential. The event will be preceded by a ‘Leadership Talk’ to be delivered by the same mentors.

Register at the link below!

04

Break The Ice

The Alumni Student Mentorship Program under SARC will be hosting the second Break The Ice session during Alumination. This session gives the Mentees an opportunity to interact freely with their Mentors over lunch and build a great rapport with them.

05

Mock Interviews and Group discussions

Mock Interviews and Group discussions: "One important key to success is self-confidence. To self-confidence, is preparation.” Facing an interview panel for the first time while being encumbered with expectations can be an unnerving experience but only for the uninitiated. With the alumni roleplaying as your interviewers, get a feel of the actual recruitment process and sail smoothly through your actual placements.

Register at the link below

06

Tech and RnD Expo

Tech and RnD Expo: SARC in association with the Technical Council, IIT Bombay and UGAC, IIT Bombay will be organising a Tech and R&D exposition to provide a platform for student and research projects to be showcased in front of students as well as the alumni of the Institute, to bolster the spirit of actively pursuing technical projects in the Institute.

07

Panel Discussion

The recipe to perfect decision making involves a thorough scrutiny of contrasting perspectives and diverging viewpoints. To facilitate a free exchange of opinions, SARC is organising a panel discussion focusing on the dichotomy between careers in core and non-core sectors and resolution of pressing problems that students face in the institute. The panel will be constituted by student(name), Professor(name) and two of our alumni(names).

08

Entrepreneurship Talk

“Dare to Dream. Dare to Excel.”
Ingenious. Intuitive. Industrious. Innovative- these are just some of the skills that hide within the glow of a successful entrepreneur. Join the magnanimous and mesmerizing Amit Kumar Agarwal, the Founder and CEO of NoBroker.com and gasp in disbelief how he trod the paths from engineering to entrepreneurship in the series, “Entrepreneurial Epics”

01

Coming full circle & Beyond the Horizon

Not having a recipe for success hasn’t stopped people from becoming the best proverbial chefs. Coming Full Circle celebrates the illustrious career of the Torrent Networking Technology Founder and CEO, a holder of multiple patents, and recipient of the 2016 Distinguished Alumnus Award, Dr. Hemant Kanakia.
